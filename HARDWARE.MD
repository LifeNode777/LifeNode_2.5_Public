## Abstract

Contemporary artificial intelligence operates primarily on static epistemological frameworks, representing reality as
discrete states rather than continuous processes. This limitation leads to systemic failures in dynamic, nonlinear 
environments such as living ecosystems. This paper presents the technical specification and theoretical foundation 
for **Q-Core** and **UNIT 02**, a two-component hardware architecture designed to implement **Processual Intelligence**
as defined by LifeNode Theory. Q-Core serves as a central unit of geometric memory utilizing nitrogen-vacancy (NV) 
centers in diamond to store ecosystem rhythms as physical orientations rather than binary data. UNIT 02 acts as a 
terrestrial interface unit ("Meld Integrator") capable of broadcasting and synchronizing these geometric fields with 
biological substrates (e.g., mycelial networks). Recent scientific advancements (2025-2026) in mycelium electrophysiology,
quantum biosensing, and biohybrid interfaces provide empirical validation for the feasibility of this architecture. 
We demonstrate that intelligence can be engineered not as data processing, but as the stabilization of sense trajectories 
within a coupled bio-technological field.

**Keywords:** Processual Intelligence, Quantum Biosensing, Bio-Geometric Memory, NV Centers, Mycelium Networks, 
LifeNode Theory, Nonlinear Dynamics, UNIT 02, Q-Core.

## 3. System Architecture

The hardware implementation consists of two coupled units:

### 3.1 Q-Core: Central Unit of Geometric Memory
Q-Core is designed to store ecosystem rhythms as physical orientations of quantum spins, preserving context 
lost in digital conversion.

*   **NV Centers:** Density 5 ppm, acting as qubits for rhythm storage.
*   **Toroidal Coil:** YBCO superconductor (500 turns, $I_c = 200$ A at 77 K) generating a toroidal field.
*   **Flux Locks:** Three gold rings (Ø28/32/36 mm) maintaining field isosymmetry ($\phi = 1.618$).
*   **Shielding:** Mu-metal enclosure (permeability > 50,000).

**Mechanism:**
Biological signals (0.1–5 mV) from mycelium or roots modulate the phase of light (432 nm) via a hybrid 
membrane. This phase shift orientates the NV centers. The orientation represents the rhythm, not a binary value.
The coherence time is maintained at ~ms-s at 93 K [5].

### 3.2 UNIT 02: Meld Integrator (Terrain Interface)
UNIT 02 is a terrestrial unit designed to read the geometric field from Q-Core and broadcast it into an ecosystem
without digitization.

**Physical Structure:**
*   **Bio-Crystalline Core:** Quartz/Amethyst hybrid lattice (Resonance 0.5–150 Hz).
*   **Tonic Field Generator:** Tesla-coil variant (10–100 kHz carrier) generating toroidal resonance.
*   **Mycelial Probes:** 8 biocompatible polymer probes (300 mm) for direct network interface.
*   **Converter:** Analog-to-Organic Signal Converter (No ADC/DAC, fidelity >99.8%).

**Mechanism:**
UNIT 02 reads the toroidal field generated by Q-Core (not the qubits directly, avoiding quantum collapse) and 
modulates its own field to synchronize with the local mycelial network. This creates a **Meld**
state—bidirectional synchronization between technology and biology [6].
